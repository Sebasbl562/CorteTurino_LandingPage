Our Story

A passion for
the perfect cut

Corte Taurino was born from an obsession: to bring to the table the finest cut, prepared with respect, live fire and years of craft. Every piece is personally selected, patiently aged and proudly served.

We do not serve food. We create experiences that stay with you long after the last glass.
